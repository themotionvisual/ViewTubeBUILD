## YouTube Tamagotchi (Core Concept)
A living creator companion where channel health maps to pet health. The creator "cares" for it by executing real channel actions.

### Core Loop
- Feed = publish Shorts
- Train = publish long-form episodes
- Walk = reply to comments/community posts
- Clean = fix metadata/thumbnail/SEO debt
- Heal = complete AI-recommended retention or CTR actions
- Sleep/Check-in streak = daily dashboard login + routine review

### Progression
- XP from analytics goals (CTR, retention, watch-time velocity)
- Evolutions unlocked by milestone consistency, not vanity spikes
- Personality skins based on content niche/style

### Gameplay + Creator Ops Fusion
- Pet mood tied to strategy adherence
- Missed routines reduce momentum score
- Daily quests generated by Oracle recommendations
- Weekly boss battle = growth goal checkpoint
